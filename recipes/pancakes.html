Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter
that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle
or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter

These Pancakes are easy to make and are soft and very fluffy These pancakes aren’t
too sweet and are lightly scented with vanilla. They are delicious and are easily
adapted to what you have in your kitchen.Let’s do this!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, (spooned and leveled)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil
  • Assorted toppings, such as butter, maple syrup, confectioners' sugar,
    honey, jams, preserves, sweetened whipped cream, or chocolate syrup

Super easy instructions for preparing delicious Pancakes

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees; have a baking sheet or heatproof platter ready
    to keep cooked pancakes warm in the oven. In a small bowl, whisk together flour,
    sugar, baking powder, and salt; set aside.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together milk, butter (or oil), and egg. Add dry ingredients
    to milk mixture; whisk until just moistened (do not overmix; a few small lumps are fine).

  3. Heat a large skillet (nonstick or cast-iron) or griddle over medium. Fold a sheet of paper towel
    in half, and moisten with oil; carefully rub skillet with oiled paper towel.

  4. For each pancake, spoon 2 to 3 tablespoons of batter onto skillet, using the back of the spoon
    to spread batter into a round (you should be able to fit 2 to 3 in a large skillet).

  5. Cook until surface of pancakes have some bubbles and a few have burst, 1 to 2 minutes.
    Flip carefully with a thin spatula, and cook until browned on the underside, 1 to 2 minutes more.
    Transfer to a baking sheet or platter; cover loosely with aluminum foil, and keep warm in oven.
    Continue with more oil and remaining batter. (You'll have 12 to 15 pancakes.)
    Serve warm, with desired toppings.

Congratulations! you have made these delicious Pancakes, now you can enjoy them with a cup of coffee.
